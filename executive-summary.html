Executive Summary

Overview of Outstanding Universal Value and nomination context.

Context

This chapter presents a concise overview of the Ustyurt Landscapes and Aran Hunting Traps nomination, summarizing location, scope, and key values.

Outstanding Universal Value

The nominated property combines archaeological hunting structures and plateau landscapes that collectively demonstrate long-term adaptation of human communities to arid environments.

Scope of the Nomination

The nomination includes property boundaries, buffer zones, legal protection, management frameworks, and supporting documentation for evaluation.
