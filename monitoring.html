Monitoring

Indicators and procedures for ongoing site oversight.

Monitoring Framework

Monitoring combines field inspection, photographic records, and geospatial tracking to evaluate property condition over time.

Core Indicators

Indicators include structural integrity of arans, disturbance events, visitor pressure, and environmental change factors.

Reporting Cycle

Monitoring results are consolidated into periodic technical reports used to guide management decisions and corrective actions.
