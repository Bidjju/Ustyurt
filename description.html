Description

Physical, cultural, and environmental characteristics of the property.

Landscape Character

The Ustyurt Plateau is defined by broad escarpments, desert-steppe plains, and extensive visual continuity that frames the archaeological system of arans.

Aran Hunting Traps

Arans are dry-stone constructions laid out across key terrain corridors and represent major evidence of organized hunting practices.

Attributes of Significance

Key attributes include spatial distribution, construction logic, setting integrity, and legibility of relationships between the traps and the wider plateau.
